## 💰 Budget Calculation Logic

### **Ghana Tax Rates** (Simplified)
- **₵0 - ₵365**: 0% (Tax-free threshold)
- **₵366 - ₵720**: 5%
- **₵721 - ₵2,160**: 10%
- **₵2,161 - ₵4,320**: 17.5%
- **₵4,321+**: 25%

### **Allocation Strategy**
- **Needs**: 45-60% (based on lifestyle)
  - Rent/Accommodation: 45% of needs
  - Utilities: 15% of needs
  - Transportation: 20% of needs
  - Food & Groceries: 20% of needs

- **Wants**: 15-35% (based on lifestyle)
  - Entertainment: 30% of wants
  - Dining Out: 25% of wants
  - Shopping: 20% of wants
  - Hobbies: 15% of wants

- **Savings**: 20-40% (based on goals)
  - Emergency Fund: 40% of savings
  - Investments: 35% of savings
  - Goal-specific: 25% of savings

## 🎯 Financial Health Scoring

The app calculates a comprehensive financial health score (0-100) based on:

- **Savings Rate** (30 points): Percentage of income saved
- **Needs Ratio** (25 points): Essential expenses vs income
- **Housing Costs** (20 points): Rent/accommodation percentage
- **Emergency Fund** (15 points): Months of expenses covered
- **Budget Balance** (10 points): Total allocation efficiency
